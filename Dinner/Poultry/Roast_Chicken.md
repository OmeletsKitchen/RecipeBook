## Roast Chicken Rub

# Ingredients

    1 tbsp each of kosher (coarse) salt, fresh ground black pepper, ground cumin, and ground coriander
    3 large cloves of fresh garlic, minced or pressed
    3 tbsp of EV olive oil

1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
2. In a small bowl, combine all rub ingredients.
3. Place the whole chicken breast down in a glass baking dish.
4. Cover the entire outside surface of the chicken with the rub. Then, get under the skin and coat the meat directly. Use all the rub.
5.Put that shit in the oven.
6. Cook for 20 mins per pound. If you have a meat thermometer, make sure it reads at least 165 F inserted into the thickest part of the thigh.
7. Serve with rice (or couscous) and vegetables.
